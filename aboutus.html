Trail Guardians

About Us

Trail Guardians is a volunteer driven non-profit organization based out of Manitoba that is dedicated to preserving, restoring, and enhancing the natural beauty of our province's incredible trail systems! Our passionate team of volunteers works hard to keep trails thriving and accessible for everyone. With every step we take, we hope to help more people connect with the breathtaking landscapes that make Manitoba so special!

Recycling

As part of our commitment to protecting Manitoba's natural beauty, we collect all litter found along the trails and partner with like-minded organizations to recycle and repurpose whenever possible. With our local action, we are proud to be part the global effort to reduce waste and preserve natural ecosystems, creating a cleaner and more sustainable world for future generations!

Volunteer

Love the outdoors? Want to help make a difference? Join our team of volunteers and assist us in our mission to help keep Manitoba's trails beautiful! Head to our Contact Us page to get started today!

How to help

We now accept donations to support our mission! Donations go towards travel costs, tools, and keeping our team fueled in the field. We also accept tools and food to help support our team! Contact us if you are wanting to lend us your support!
